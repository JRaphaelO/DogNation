DogNation

Adopt a dog, with just one click.

DogNation is a non-profit organization, with the goal of making it easier for people to adopt dogs. Come talk to us at one of our social networks.

History

DogNation is an OSCIP (Civil Society Organization of Public Interest) that was founded in January 2020, with the objective of providing homes for helpless dogs in Paraíba.

DogNation has a shelter, located in the district of Mangabeira / João Pessoa, always seeking to act preventively, following the following pillars: care, affection and adoption. It also acts by assisting ONGs and independent protectors, as a means of communicating them with people.
